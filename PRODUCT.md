# Product

## Register
product

## Users
Race engineers, analysts, and enthusiastic fans needing immediate insights into F1 race telemetry data in near real-time.

## Product Purpose
Production-grade F1 monitoring system inspired by industrial pitwall telemetry. It ingests, processes, and presents race data accurately and instantly for tactical decision-making.

## Brand Personality
Technical, industrial, precise, high-contrast, data-heavy, professional.

## Anti-references
- No playful SaaS illustrations or pastel "creamy" color palettes.
- No heavy rounded corners or bubble-like shapes.
- No generic AI-generated dark mode aesthetics (e.g., violet neon glows).
- No unnecessary animations that distract from data.

## Design Principles
- Uncompromising clarity: The data is the hero, minimize interface chrome.
- Industrial utility: Form follows the function of rapid technical analysis.
- Monospace where it counts: Tabular numerals and data consistency over decorative typography.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast for legibility in varied lighting conditions (e.g., bright race track pitwall or dim analysis room).
- Clear state indicators that do not rely solely on color.
